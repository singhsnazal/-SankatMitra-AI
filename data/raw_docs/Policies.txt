Company Policies (Summary)

1) Working Hours
- Standard working hours: 9 hours/day (including breaks).
- Typical shift: 10:00 AM - 7:00 PM IST.
- Flexible hours may be allowed depending on manager approval and project needs.

2) Work From Home (WFH) / Hybrid Policy
- Most teams follow a hybrid model (2-3 days office).
- Fully remote roles depend on project/client approval.
- WFH requests should be communicated to manager/HR in advance.

3) Leave Policy
- Paid leaves: 18 per year (pro-rated).
- Sick leaves: 6 per year.
- Casual leaves: 6 per year.
- Leaves should be applied in the portal and approved by reporting manager.

4) Holidays
- Company follows a standard holiday calendar.
- Optional holidays may be available depending on region.

5) Notice Period
- Standard notice period: 30 days (varies by designation and contract).
- Early release is subject to manager approval and project handover completion.

6) Probation Period
- Probation: 3 to 6 months depending on role.
- Performance review happens during probation.

7) Salary & Benefits
- Salary is credited monthly (usually last working day).
- Health insurance coverage provided for employee (and dependents as per plan).
- Performance bonus/variable pay is project dependent.

8) Code of Conduct
- Professional behavior expected at workplace.
- Harassment and discrimination are strictly prohibited.

9) Background Verification
- Candidate documents may be verified post-offer or during onboarding.
- Any mismatch may lead to offer withdrawal.
